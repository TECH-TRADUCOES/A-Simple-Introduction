# A Simple Introduction

My name is Daniel. I am 30 years old and I work as a mechanic. I live in Chicago, in the United States. I live in a small house near a park.

I usually wake up at 6:30 in the morning. I drink coffee and eat eggs and toast for breakfast. After that, I drive to work. I work in a car repair shop.

I like my job because I enjoy fixing cars and helping people. I work from Monday to Friday. I usually finish work at 5:00 in the afternoon.

In my free time, I like listening to music, watching movies, and playing football with my friends. My favorite food is pizza and my favorite drink is orange juice.

On weekends, I often visit my family or relax at home. I like my life because it is simple and comfortable.
